Chat System

Is it secure?

Yes, you can trust that the Javascript being downloaded is not being tampered with. This page itself is served by Restore The Fourth SF Bay Area via TLS encrypted (i.e. served via HTTPS). This prevents malicious software from being delivered to your browser.

The chat system itself makes encrypted HTTPS requests to our connection manager , which will make an SSL/TLS encrypted connection to our XMPP server.

We strongly reccomend you use the OTR functionality to further protect the contents of your communications.

How do I get an account?

Accounts are only available to members of Restore the Fourth SF. Please attend a meeting or otherwise get involved to get an account.

Limitations

There appears to be a bug in the implementation of OTR in the web client which prevents OTR conversations between web clients and desktop/mobile clients. OTR conversations amongst Adium/Pidgin/ChatSecure or webclients work fine.
